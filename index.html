Exploring Social Media's Impact on Public Perceptions

Discover Our Vibrant World

In the digital age, social media has become a powerful force influencing public perceptions, particularly in the context of ongoing crises. This survey delves into the intricate ways in which platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram shape our understanding of various crises.

The immediacy and accessibility of information on social media can either provide a more nuanced perspective or contribute to the polarization of opinions. As users engage with diverse content, the survey aims to unravel the extent to which social media serves as a double-edged sword, both informing and potentially manipulating public perceptions during turbulent times.

The survey explores how social media algorithms curate and prioritize content related to crises, impacting the narratives that gain prominence in users' feeds. With the potential for misinformation and the rapid spread of rumors, the survey seeks to highlight the challenges in discerning reliable information from the noise. Additionally, it investigates the role of social media in amplifying certain voices and perspectives, potentially shaping the public discourse in ways that may not accurately reflect the complexities of ongoing crises.
